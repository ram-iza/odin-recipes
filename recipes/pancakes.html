Pancakes

Home

Description

Soft yogurt pancakes, to start your day in the best way!

Ingredients

  • Yogurt 100g
  • 1 Egg
  • Honey 1 tsp
  • Flour 100g
  • Baking Powder 1 tsp

Steps

  1. In a large bowl mix yogurt, egg and honey.
  2. Sift the flour and baking powder while mixing with the wet ingredients.
  3. Heat a pan and cook the pancakes on one side, flip it when the dough starts to bubble.
  4. When the pancakes are done cooking on both sides serve them with your preferred toppings (e.g. fresh fruit and maple syrup)
