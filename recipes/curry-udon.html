Curry Udon

Home

Description

Good for the cold, good for the soul.

Ingredients

  • Udon (fresh or frozen) 1 pack
  • 1 Carrot
  • 1 Onion
  • 1 block Japanese Curry Roux
  • 1 tbs Dashi powder

Steps

  1. Chop all the veggies and stir fry in a pot with oil.
  2. Add water and bring to a boil.
  3. When the veggies are soft turn off the heat and add the block of curry roux.
  4. When the roux melts completely turn the heat back on and add the udon.
  5. Serve hot with a sprinkle of finely chopped leek and/or sesame seeds on top.
